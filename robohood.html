Robohood

Unreal Engine C++ Blueprints Multiplayer

RoboHood was a group project made during the 3rd year at London Metropolitan University. The game is a third person lan multiplayer, one versus one robot themed shooter. Players can pick up rockets, bombs and health aswell as use launchpads and teleporters to defeat their enemy and see their score on the scoreboard.

Features

  • Server Search & Creation.
  • Multiplayer Lobby With Character & Map Selection.
  • Projectile Based Weapons.
  • Multiple Weapons.
  • Server & Client Replication.
  • Score System.
