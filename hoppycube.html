Hoppy Cube

Unity C# Mobile

Hoppy Cube is a super casual one button mobile game currently in development. The goal of the game is to hopp from platform to platform by judging the correct power to give the jump, while avoiding different obstables.

Hoppy Cube will be released late 2022.
