Sumo Tank

Unity C# AI

Sumo Tank was a solo project made during my 3rd year at London Metropolitan University for the Ai in games module. The goal of Sumo Tank is to hit your enemy off the edge of the platform while avoiding mines falling from the sky, the more you get hit the further hits will push you.

Features

  • Unique AI Tank Behaviour.
  • Customisable Rounds & Players.
  • Two Player Controller & Keyboard Support.
  • Projectile Trajectory Calculations.
  • Game Manager Handles Creation & Respawning of Tanks.
