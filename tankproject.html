Tank Project

The Tank Physics Project was a solo project created during the 3rd year at London Metropolitan University for my final year project module. The project was designed to challenge me and to gain knowledge on vehicle based physics for games.

Features

  • Custom Raycast Suspension.
  • Friction & Drive Force Calculation.
  • Tank Track & Component Animation.
  • Customisable Parameters.
